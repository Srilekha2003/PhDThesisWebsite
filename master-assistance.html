Essay Writing

An essay is used to assess the strength of your critical thinking and your ability to put that thinking into an academic written form. No matter what we’re writing, Research Proposal in Tamil Nadu audience should influence your language.

Essay writing needed to be in five parts of paragraphs. Each paragraph represents their focusing on stages:

First part: Introduction – To get introduced to the topic and address the specification of essay as a statement to clearly present your arguments.

Second part: Present the strongest point of your statement with a transitional sentence from the introduction.

Third part: Present the second most compelling argument in support of your essay.

Fourth part: Present your weakest argument in support of your essay and conclude this section with your final point.

Fifth part: Conclusion – Summarize the previous three major points and provide a final statement.
